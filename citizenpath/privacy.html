Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CitizenPath ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the CitizenPath mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

2. Automatically Collected Information

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google) to collect anonymous usage data. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App session duration and feature usage (e.g., which test version was taken, test score)
  • Language and region settings
  • Crash reports

This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. It helps us understand how users interact with the App and improve it over time.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the advertising ID on iOS) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your settings and Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

We request your permission before enabling personalized advertising in accordance with Apple's guidelines.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob

We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for individuals preparing for the U.S. Naturalization Civics Test. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Data Retention

Analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's standard retention policies. We do not have direct control over data stored by Firebase or AdMob on Google's servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
